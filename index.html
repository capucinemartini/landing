My work

Fame is not the panache of talented painters. Some great painters have made their mark through their work without receiving the recognition they deserve. In the criteria I have chosen, I have tried to present 10 painters from the 15th to the 20th century, who are no longer active, who do not benefit from an important notoriety at present, and whose works are therefore, in my opinion, underrated. This is why I am launching my site to promote my works.
